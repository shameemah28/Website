FLAGSHIP EVENT

The Future FinTech Champion (FFC) Programme, spearheaded by the Mauritius Africa FinTech Hub and supported by the Mauritius Commercial Bank (MCB),
is a dynamic half-year initiative designed to cultivate the next generation of FinTech leaders.
This programme immerses participants in the vibrant Mauritian FinTech ecosystem,
offering hands-on experience through workshops, industry events, regulatory roundtables, and internships.

Participants, known as Future FinTech Champions, gain valuable insights into emerging technologies,
industry opportunities, and regulatory challenges.
They also develop critical skills such as communication, problem-solving, and leadership.
As ambassadors for the FinTech sector, FFCs are encouraged to organize awareness-raising events
and initiatives at their home universities, engage in research, and contribute opinion articles,
further enhancing their industry readiness and professional growth.
