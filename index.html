FLAGSHIP EVENT

Our vision is to establish a pioneering Shark Tank-inspired flagship event where the brightest young minds consisting of university students and SMEs come together to design transformative business plans and strategies for the future of banking and first-time employee onboarding. Through this platform, teams of 3 participants maximum will select one of the proposed themes, elaborate on their ideas, write up a business plan, and pitch their business concepts to a panel of investors who will also be the jurists. We envision of encouraging a culture of innovation and entrepreneurship, where university students and SMEs can creatively address key challenges in the banking industry, such as revolutionizing mobile trading and investment, enhancing employee onboarding experiences, and driving green banking solutions.

By encouraging participants to pitch their ideas in a competitive yet collaborative environment, we aim to nurture their potential, and inspire the development of solutions that contribute to global financial inclusivity, sustainable business practices, and cutting-edge technological advancements. This event will serve as a launchpad for future leaders to shape a more adaptive, sustainable, and customer-centric banking industry, ultimately driving long-term economic growth and societal well- being.
